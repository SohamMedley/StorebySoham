The Story Behind Our Creative Journey

Meet Soham

Hello! I'm Soham, a passionate graphic designer and video editor with over 8 years of experience in the creative industry. My journey began as a freelance designer working with small businesses, which eventually led me to collaborate with major brands and creative agencies.

Throughout my career, I've developed a unique style that combines modern aesthetics with functional design principles. My work has been recognized in several design competitions and featured in creative publications.

I created dzynsbysoham and vzualsbysoham to share my expertise and resources with fellow creators, helping them elevate their projects and streamline their workflows.
